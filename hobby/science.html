Just Rocks

Outer Space

This was my favorite part of Earth Science because it was about astronomical theories

Although I still learned about rocks(meteors), there were other interesting stuff like black holes and galaxies.

I also learned about other theoretical stuff like dark matter.

Biology

Chemistry

The Table

The periodic table wasn't actually dreaded because I didn't have to memorize it.

In fact it helped understand valence electrons for elements.

Organic Chemistry

This was my favorite part of chemistry because the prefixes that make up confusing structures actually made sense.

I learned a lot of stuff from organic chemistry including actual real life things made from these carbon chains.

These things include mustard gas, amino acid, and different alchohols.
